Who is the Kiwanis Family?


The Kiwanis family is a number of organizations aimed at improving the community. The Bradford area has an assortment of organizations from the Kiwanis family. Below you will find a short description about each organization in Bradford.



Kiwanis International

Kiwanis International is most-likely the first name you see when first introduced to Kiwanis.





Kiwanis Key Club

Kiwanis Key Club helps high school students get involved in the community. In Key Club, students help Kiwanis fulfil the purpose of serving the children of the world.





Kiwanis Aktion Club

Kiwanis Aktion Club strives to improve the lives of disabled adults. Aktion Club improves the lives of disabled adults by working with multiple other organizations such as the YMCA to hold events and programs.





Kiwanis Builders Club

Kiwanis Builders Club helps junior high and middle school students get involved in the community by holding events and completing projects. Builders Club's mission is to build leaders out of our young students today.
